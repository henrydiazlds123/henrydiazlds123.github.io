About Lupien Tree



Over 95 years ago, Waldo E. Lupien started W.E. Lupien & Sons, now known as Lupien Tree & Landscape. Waldo E Lupien 1897 - 1990, A member of the Lupien family and one of the founding fathers of the Massachusetts Arborist Association. Waldo was born on April 25, 1897 and lived a full life until his passing on September 5, 1990 at 93 years old.

Lupien Tree & Landscape continues to operate as a Family owned, Professional Service Business that conforms to all of the requirements, set forth in the industry, and is Fully Licensed, Bonded & Insured.
